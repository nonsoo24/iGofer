About

iGofer is a market place that connects individuals, businesses and organisations with qualified service experts in areas such as handyman services, virtual assistant, automobile mechanics, event management, errands, academics

How it works for clients

Hire

Fill our hire form giving a detailed
description of your needs

Pre-view

An agent pre-views your form to
ascertain your needs and right personnel for it

Assign

An agent assigns you an available
iGofer personnel suitable to fix your needs

Make payment

Make payment for service rendered
through iGofer escrow payment system

Job completion

An iGofer personnel assigned
to you completes your job

Review

An iGofer agent confirms
the work was done accurately

How it works for service providers

 

Create Account

Create a free account with your photos,
portfolio, certificates, description and qualifications.

Get paired with clients

Get paired with clients when they
request your services and start earning

Grow

Grow your business, boost your
profile and reach out to more Customers
